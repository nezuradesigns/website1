Ladies Own Transport Services isn’t just a company. It’s a movement. Founded by Joanie Fredericks in 2018, it was born from a deep desire to reclaim freedom for women in a world where safety isn't guaranteed.

At Ladies Own Transport Services, we go beyond driving lessons — we build confidence, independence, and long-term empowerment for women in our communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Ladies Own Transport Services?

LOTS welcomes women of all ages and backgrounds who want to gain their driver's license and empower themselves through driving. Whether you’re a beginner or need extra practice, we are here to support you.

What types of driving lessons do you offer?

We provide comprehensive driving lessons tailored to individual needs — from basic learner training to advanced road skills. Our instructors focus on safe, confident, and responsible driving.

Are your instructors women too?

Yes! LOTS is proud to be women-led and women-staffed. Our founder Joanie Fredericks, Chief Instructor Eileen Conrad, and instructor Rowena are all passionate about empowering women through driving education.

How do I sign up for lessons?

You can contact us via the form on our website or call us directly. We will discuss your goals and arrange a suitable lesson plan. LOTS aims to be accessible and supportive to every learner.

Do you support learners from underprivileged communities?

Absolutely. LOTS is dedicated to empowering women, especially those from underserved communities, by providing affordable, quality driving lessons and support throughout their learning journey.

Still have questions?

If you can’t find the answer you’re looking for, please get in touch with us. We’re happy to help you get started on your driving journey!
