20-Hour Driving Package

Comprehensive Test Readiness

Learning to drive is a major milestone — and it doesn’t have to feel intimidating. At Ladies Own Transport Services, our 20-Hour Driving Package is designed to take you from beginner to test-ready with confidence. This comprehensive program offers the time, support, and structure you need to build strong driving habits and pass your K53 test with ease.

What to Expect:

This package is ideal for learners who want a well-paced, supportive training experience. You’ll receive 20 hours of in-depth, hands-on instruction tailored to your learning style and experience level. Each session is focused on skill-building, confidence, and safety — ensuring you progress at a steady, comfortable pace.

Who Is This Package For?

  • Complete Beginners – No prior driving experience? No problem.
  • Learners Who Prefer Structure – A step-by-step program that ensures steady progress.
  • Drivers Needing Confidence – Patient instruction to ease nerves and build comfort behind the wheel.
  • First-Time Pass Seekers – Covers everything needed for a successful K53 test on your first attempt.

Key Benefits:

  • 20 Hours of Instructor-Led Driving Lessons
  • Gradual, Skill-Building Progression
  • Comprehensive Coverage of the K53 Curriculum
  • Focused Practice on Maneuvers and Parking
  • Expert Test Preparation Techniques
  • Confidence Boosting Through Repetition and Guidance

Why Choose the 20-Hour Package?

This package is our most thorough and supportive offering, created for learners who want to be fully equipped before stepping into a testing center. With personalized attention, practical driving exposure, and expert instruction, you'll finish the program with the confidence and readiness you need to succeed — not just for the test, but for lifelong safe driving.

Take the wheel with certainty — choose the 20-Hour Driving Package and let us guide you every step of the way.
