Ladies Own Transport Services

Ladies Own Transport Services isn’t just a company — it’s a movement. Born in 2018 out of Joanie Fredericks’ deep desire to disrupt the status quo, Ladies Own was created as a bold answer to a brutal question: How do we reclaim freedom in a world that often makes women feel unsafe?

The answer? We take the wheel. And we don’t let go.

Rooted in Cape Town, South Africa, our mission is simple but powerful: to create a safe, empowering space where women can learn to drive — and unlearn the fear, trauma, and psychological conditioning that told them they couldn’t.

What Drives Us

We believe that mobility is power. That a steering wheel can be a tool of liberation. And that when women learn to drive, they’re not just navigating roads — they’re reshaping their futures.

  • Empowerment over fear
  • Personal growth over limitation
  • Sisterhood over silence
  • Passion over passivity
More Than a Driving School

We are a force — a crew of committed women working to dismantle gender-based violence by giving women the tools to move freely, confidently, and safely in the world.

We don’t just deliver lessons. We deliver courage, confidence, and community.

Join the movement. Let’s drive change — together.
